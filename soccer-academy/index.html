WELCOME TO TOMADA SOCCER ACADEMY

Tomada soccer academy was made for every young girl and boy with the potential and desire to become a professional footballer. Tom Ada soccer academy was made to help the youths accomplish their dreams by giving them the training and push to become a world-class players someday.

The Academy

The academy is divided into 3 age ranges 7-10, 11-14, and 15-18. These age ranges have teams for boys and girls. The academy participated in tournaments to expose our young athletes. Our qualified coaching team will deliver football and analysis sessions, providing players with the right environment to develop their skills and techniques ready to showcase their talents in front of professional scouts, coaches and agents. More than just a soccer Academy we are a family. Be a part of our family and support our young stars by buying tickets to our games and attending them.
