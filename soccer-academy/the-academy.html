TOMADA SOCCER ACADEMY

Tomada soccer academy was made for every young girl and boy with the potential and desire to become a professional footballer. Tomada soccer academy was made to help the youths accomplish their dreams by giving them the training and push to become a world-class players someday. We have world-class coaches who will be there to support and train our young athletes. If you believe you or your son/daughter has the ability, talent and desire to play the game professionally, then joining Tomada Soccer Academy could prove to be the most important, and the best, decision of his/her life. The male first team is made up of our young boys from 14-18 and they participate in state and national level competitions. The female first team is made up of our young girls from 14-18 and they participate in state and national level competitions.
